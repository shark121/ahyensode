About Blobmixer

Blobmixer is a toy for creating and sharing 3D blobs. Built with Three.js, custom GLSL shaders, and plenty of simplex noise.

Use the Remix button to randomize all blob parameters — noise, colors, geometry, and distortion. Press Random for a completely new blob, or navigate with the left/right sides of the screen.

Inspired by the original Blobmixer by 14islands.

Clone crafted with Three.js & GSAP.
